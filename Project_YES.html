Aim And Objective
There is a lack of mental health awareness and understanding among the youth regarding mental health and emotional support for self and others. Along with lack of knowledge, there is also misinformation, popularised with inaccurate representation of mental health on social media. Additionally, there is a lack of techniques used among the youth when dealing with emotional problems as well as providing emotional support. Thus, the Healing Dove Foundation and the ICHARS (Institute of Clinical Hypnosis and Related Sciences) Support Foundation have combined efforts to provide training programs in emotional support for self and others to the youth. The aim is to create a generation of young mental health advocates capable of providing first aid mental health care for themselves and those around them.
