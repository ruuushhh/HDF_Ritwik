Aim And Objective
An initiative undertaken by ICHARS (Institute of Clinical Hypnosis and Related Sciences) Support Foundation and Healing Dove Foundation. The objective of this initiative is to reach out to other NGOs who are providing services to the marginalised sections of the society. The project aims at empowering the staff & employees of the other NGOs through training and mentorship in Emotional First Aid Support for their beneficiaries. Along with this the project aims at connecting mental health practitioners trained by ICHARS Support Foundation, with the other NGOs to provide mental health support to their beneficiaries.
